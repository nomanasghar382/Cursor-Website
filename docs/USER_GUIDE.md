# User Guide

## Shopping

1. Browse the catalog at `/products`
2. Use AI Studio at `/ai` for semantic search and recommendations
3. Add items to cart and proceed to `/checkout`
4. Complete payment with Stripe

## Account

Sign in at `/login` to access:

- **Dashboard** — order summary, rewards, recommendations
- **Orders** — tracking and order history
- **Addresses** — shipping addresses
- **Security** — password, MFA, sessions
- **AI Preferences** — personalization settings

## Email verification

After registration, check your inbox for a verification link pointing to `/verify-email`.

## Password reset

Use **Forgot password** on the login page. Reset links open `/reset-password`.

## Newsletter

Subscribe from the homepage footer. Manage preferences from account settings.

## Support

Visit `/support` or email support@novaex.ai.
